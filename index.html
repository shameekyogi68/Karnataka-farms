About Monstera Deliciosa

Native to the tropical forests of Southern Mexico and Panama, the Monstera Deliciosa has become a global icon of modern interior design. Its unique fenestrations (leaf holes) develop as the plant matures, creating an ever-changing living sculpture.

At Karnataka Farms, every Monstera is grown from carefully selected cuttings in our controlled nursery environment. We acclimate each plant to indoor conditions for a minimum of 4 weeks before shipping, ensuring it arrives ready to thrive in your home.

Care Instructions

  • Light: Place in bright, indirect sunlight. An east-facing window is ideal.
  • Water: Water thoroughly when the top 1-2 inches of soil feel dry. Reduce in winter.
  • Humidity: Prefers 60%+ humidity. Mist occasionally or use a pebble tray.
  • Fertilizer: Feed with balanced liquid fertilizer every 2 weeks during growing season (March-September).
  • Pruning: Remove yellowing leaves at the base to redirect energy to new growth.

Plant Care Guide

Master the art of plant parenting with our expert-curated care guides. From watering schedules to pest control, we've got you covered.

Watering Wisdom

The most common mistake is overwatering. Most indoor plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Stick your finger 1-2 inches into the soil — if it feels dry, it's time to water. Always use room temperature water and ensure pots have drainage holes.

Light Levels

Bright indirect light is the sweet spot for most tropical houseplants. Direct afternoon sun can scorch leaves, while too little light leads to leggy growth. Rotate your plants weekly for even growth on all sides.

Humidity & Temperature

Most indoor plants are tropical natives and prefer 50-70% humidity. In dry climates or AC-heavy spaces, mist plants regularly, use a pebble tray, or invest in a humidifier. Keep temperatures between 18-30°C for optimal growth.

Fertilizing

Feed your plants during the growing season (March-September) with a balanced liquid fertilizer diluted to half strength. Reduce feeding in winter when growth naturally slows. Always water before fertilizing to prevent root burn.

Our Commitment to the Earth

At Karnataka Farms, sustainability isn't a buzzword — it's the foundation of everything we do.

  • Organic Farming: We use natural compost and organic pest control. No chemical fertilizers or synthetic pesticides ever touch our soil.
  • Water Conservation: Our nursery employs drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting to minimize water waste.
  • Biodegradable Packaging: All shipping materials are either recyclable or biodegradable. We never use plastic wraps.
