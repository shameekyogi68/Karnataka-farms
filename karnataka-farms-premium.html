About Monstera Deliciosa

Native to the tropical forests of Southern Mexico and Panama, the Monstera Deliciosa has become a global icon of modern interior design. Its unique fenestrations (leaf holes) develop as the plant matures, creating an ever-changing living sculpture.

At Karnataka Farms, we grow our Monsteras from tissue-culture stock in controlled nursery environments. Each plant is hardened off over 8 weeks before sale, ensuring it thrives in typical Indian household humidity and temperature ranges.

Care Instructions

  • Light: Place in bright, indirect sunlight. An east-facing window is ideal.
  • Water: Water thoroughly when the top 1-2 inches of soil feel dry. Reduce in winter.
  • Humidity: Prefers 60%+ humidity. Mist occasionally or use a pebble tray.
  • Fertilizer: Feed with balanced liquid fertilizer every 2 weeks during growing season (March-September).
  • Pruning: Remove yellowing leaves at the base to redirect energy to new growth.
